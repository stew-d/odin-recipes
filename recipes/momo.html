Chicken Momo

Description

Chicken Momos Recipe is a delicious and simple momos recipe filled with juicy minced chicken. The fillings for these momos are quite simple and light. Serve this momos along with chilli garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast and thigh
  • White onion
  • Spring onion
  • Garlic ginger paste
  • Cumin powder
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Tumeric
  • Momo masala
  • Coriander powder
  • Chilli powder
